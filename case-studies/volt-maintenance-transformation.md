
# Maintenance Delivery Improvement

## Company

Electric Motor Manufacturing

## Role

Maintenance Chief

## Objective

Increase equipment availability while reducing repeated failures.

## Responsibilities

- Planned preventive maintenance
- Coordinated maintenance technicians
- Managed maintenance budget
- Analysed MTTR and MTBF
- Planned commissioning activities
- Coordinated external suppliers

## Key Metrics

- MTTR
- MTBF
- Preventive Maintenance Completion
- Equipment Availability

## Lessons Learned

Recurring failures usually reveal process problems rather than equipment problems.

Improving root causes created more value than increasing maintenance workload.
